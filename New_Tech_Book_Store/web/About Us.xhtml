ABOUT US

In USA, shopping for books has always been thought of as a book-lover's hobby. The traditional bookstores, with their dreary, dusty and browser-unfriendly atmosphere, have discouraged all but the serious book-buyers.

Launched in 1980, New Tech Book Store has become the one stop destination for book lovers. We stock a wide range of books spanning genres of Fiction, Non-Fiction, Biographical, Science and Technology, and Children's books. In such a short span of time, we have become one of the biggest book stores on the Web. We have a massive customer base with more than 800,000 registered users.

We provide the following services to our members:
* Home delivery of books
* Special discounts
* Gift vouchers
